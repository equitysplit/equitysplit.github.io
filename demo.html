This is our beta algorithm that calculates equity share for team members in a startup or company.

They key variables are an individual’s annual compensation, hours worked as well as invested and contributed cash. The logic is that “hours worked” and “hourly rate” translates into individual's equity ownership and any invested/contributed cash having a 4x multiplier gets added as well. Time effort is easier to contribute than hard earned cash, hence the multiplier being added to account for it. “Date joined the project” is an important date as it is keeping track how long individual has been a part of the team. One can estimate a probation period for new members, let's say 3 and 6 months and then a 2 year, or any X years defined as a vested period.
